SketchShop

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026

1. Introduction

SketchShop is a mobile application that lets you photograph a space, describe a home improvement project, and receive an AI-generated visualization. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is handled.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum data required to operate the service:

DataSourcePurpose
Email address Provided by Google or Apple at sign-in Account identification, abuse prevention
Clerk user ID Assigned by Clerk at sign-in Internal account reference, session linking

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, payment card details, or any other personal identifiers beyond those listed above.

3. Your Photos

Photos you take or select within the app are:

  • Transmitted to our server over HTTPS for AI processing
  • Never stored on our servers — processed entirely in memory and discarded immediately after the AI-generated result is returned
  • Not shared with any third party except the AI providers listed in Section 5, solely for generating your visualization

4. How We Use Your Information

PurposeData used
Creating and maintaining your account Email address, Clerk user ID
Managing your session (free vs. paid tier, image count) Clerk user ID
Preventing abuse (duplicate accounts, rate limiting) Normalized email address
Security event logging Clerk user ID, IP address

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

ServiceRolePrivacy policy
Clerk Authentication (Google/Apple OAuth, JWT) clerk.com/privacy
Supabase Database (account records, session data) supabase.com/privacy
OpenAI Image generation (GPT-Image-1) openai.com/privacy
Anthropic AI text generation (Claude) anthropic.com/privacy
Apple In-app payments, app distribution apple.com/legal/privacy

When your photo is processed, it is sent to OpenAI and Anthropic solely to produce your visualization. These providers do not use submitted content to train their models under their standard API terms.

6. Data Retention

DataRetention period
Session records (tier, image count) 7 days from session creation, then deleted automatically
Account record (email, Clerk user ID) Until you delete your account
Security event logs 90 days
Your photos Not retained — discarded immediately after processing

7. Your Rights

You may request at any time:

  • Access: a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion: permanent deletion of your account and all associated data

To delete your account, use the "Delete My Account" option in the app's Settings screen. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
